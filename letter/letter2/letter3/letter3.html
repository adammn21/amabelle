Dear, Verena Amabelle Tracey

Dalam setiap fase hidupmu, baik yang cerah maupun yang gelap, aku akan menjadi menemanimu selalu, siap menyemangatimu dan menerima celoteh tentang masalahmu. I want you to know that I will always stay with you, my dearest one. I will be there for you during both your good times and your struggles. In a constantly changing world, having you by my side is truly a blessing. I admire your strength, but I also want you to feel comfortable being vulnerable in front of me.
